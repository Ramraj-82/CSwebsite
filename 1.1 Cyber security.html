Cyber security

  • Cyber security is a subset of infosec, it is the practice of protecting the organization systems, networks, applications, servers and programs from external cyber attack.

  • It encompasses a wide range of technologies, process and practice designed to defend against unauthorized access, attack and damage.

  • It deals with threats like hacking, malware, phishing, ransomware and other types of cyber threats that target the infra of an organization.

  • By using Firewalls, EDR, IDS, IPS SIEM tools, protect the system and data from external cyber attacks.

  • If any attack happen below are the impacted area on the organization.

  1. 💸 Financial Loss

  2. 🔓 Data Breach

  3. 📉 Reputation Damage

  4. 🕒 Business Disruption

  5. ⚖️ Legal Penalties

  6. 🔧 Recovery Costs

  7. 💼 Loss of Competitive Advantage

  8. 📉 Stock Value Drop (for public companies)

  9. 😟 Employee Impact

  10. 📑 Insurance Impact
