Information Sec OR Cyber Sec

  • It is the practice of protecting digital and physical information of the organization from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, disruption, modification, or destruction.
  • It includes not only cyber threats but also physical threats like fire, theft and environmental damages.
  • Infosec achieved by implementing security polices, controls and procedures.
  • It mainly focuses on the CIA Traid.

Common areas in InfoSec include:

  • Network Security – Securing data as it travels across networks.

  • Application Security – Making sure software is free from vulnerabilities.

  • Endpoint Security – Protecting individual devices like laptops and phones.

  • Identity and Access Management (IAM) – Controlling who can access what.

  • Security Operations – Monitoring systems, detecting threats, and responding to incidents.
